Original Members

Adam Gontier

Adam Wade Gontier is a Canadian rock musician. He is the lead singer, rhythm guitarist and main songwriter for Saint Asonia, but is best known as the former lead singer, rhythm guitarist, and founding member of the rock band Three Days Grace. He left the band in 2013.



Niel Sanderson

Neil Christopher Sanderson was born in Canada on December the 17th, 1978. He is the drummer, backing vocalist, keyboardist, and co-founder of the Candaian rock band Three Days Grace.
